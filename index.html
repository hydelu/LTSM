About this website

While I am continuously exploring the Yarra River, I'm also curious to know how other people express their thoughts and relationship with this land. Social media could be an interesting "place" to look at.

Start with some interesting Instagram posts, I use publicly available geolocation tags from the posts to identify the estimated locations in the real world and visit those places to make sound recording. Compared with the image in the original post, sound reveals more information about the place, examining the relationship between the individual and the physical location from a different perspective. The use of digital map also provides a sense of exploration.

A big "Thanks" to @4tanutgal @hannahsofiaa @click.saidrose @famymojo @m_scott for their inspiring posts.
